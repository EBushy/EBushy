About Myself

My name is Elliot Bushman, I recently graduated from UConn with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. There I was also the Chief Engineer of the UConn Formula SAE team. I now work for Ensign-Bickford Aerospace & Defense as a development engineer in the Separation and Destruction product group. We develop energetic flight termination and stage separation systems used across the aerospace and defense industry.
