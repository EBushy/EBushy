United Kingdom Part 3


Posted on July 7th 2025

After getting out of Manchester it was nice to be camping again. There was nice little campground outside of Hadfield with a large field for pitches and a little bar. I spend two nights there and spent the day doing a hike up to the site of a B29 Superfortress that had crashed shortly after the end of the second World War. The grass lands at the top of the plateau had many small streams and stripes of exposed peat crossing it. My feet took a couple good dips in the mud soaking my shoes, still the view from the top was pleasant.
