UConn Formula SAE is a self led student team that develops and builds a formula style race car. They compete at the SAE sponsored event held at Michigan International Speedway every year, this has over 120 teams from around the world.

The team with all of its members and contributors has become a monumental force in my growth over the past 4 years. For that and the opportunity to help lead the team as Chief Engineer for the 2021-2022 season I am eternally grateful.
